Spaetzle

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups (500 g) Flour
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 8 eggs
  • 3/4 cup Milk

Instructions:

  1. Add the ingredients to the mixing bowl of a stand mixer. Let mix on medium-low speed until well combined. Then let rest for about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Using a spaetzle maker or a large holed colander, press the batter through the holes of the device and into the boiling water. Let cook for 2-3 minutes and then strain from the water.
