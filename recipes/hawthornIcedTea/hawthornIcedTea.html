Hawthorn Iced Tea

Ingredients:

  • 70 g Dried hawthorn
  • 6 cups Water
  • 1/4 cup Honey, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the hawthorn and water into a pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Cover and let simmer for 45 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat and stir in the honey. Strain into a pitcher and let cool completely before transfering to the fridge to chill. Serve cold over ice.
