Mango Lassi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 cup Mango, very ripe, diced
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1-2 tbsp Sugar or Honey
  • Optional: Cardamom or Cinnamon, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place the yogurt, mango, milk and yogurt into a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Transfer to serving glasses. Optionally, garnish with cardamom or cinnamon.
