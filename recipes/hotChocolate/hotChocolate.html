Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 4 oz Chocolate, preferrably 70%
  • Sugar, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium until just starting to foam. Then reduce heat to low.
  2. Whisk in the chocolate until completely melted. Whisk continously until it reaches the desired consistency.
  3. Add sugar to taste if desired.
