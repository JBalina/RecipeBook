Butter Mochi

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Mochiko (Glutinous rice flour)
  • 600 g Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 cup Butter, melted
  • 5 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 12 oz Coconut milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 9x13 inch Baking dish.
  2. Place all ingredients into a large mixing bowl and mix together with an electric hand mixer until completely smooth. Then transfer to the greased baking dish. Bake for 1 hour.
  3. Remove from the oven and let cool to room temperature before slicing and serving.
