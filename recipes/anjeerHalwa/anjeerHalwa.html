Anjeer Halwa

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Dried figs, coarsely chopped
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/3 cup Ghee
  • 1 cup Walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Pistachios, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Ground cardamom

Instructions:

  1. Add the dried figs and water to a pot.
  2. Cook partially covered on medium heat for 20 to 25 mins or until most of the water is evaporated and the figs and super soft and mushy.
  3. With an immersion blender or a potato masher, Coarsley mash the figs and then add ghee, walnuts, pistachios, and cardamom.
  4. Cook on low-medium for 5 to 10 mins, or until it thickens to a pudding-like consistency. Serve in bowls with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
