Churros

Ingredients:

Churros:

  • 1 cup Water
  • 6 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup (140 g) Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Cinnamon Sugar Coating:

  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Combine the cinnamon sugar mixture and place into a shallow bowl.
  2. Add the water, butter, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt to a pot. Mix and bring to a boil. Once it comes to a boil, remove from heat and mix in the flour with a wooden spoon until the mixture is smooth.
  3. While the mixture cools down for a couple of minutes, place 1 1/2 inches of oil into a pot and heat to about 330 degrees.
  4. While waiting for the oil to heat, mix in the eggs to the mixture one at a time until the mixture is very smooth. Transfer the mixture to a piping bag with a large closed star piping tip, making sure there are no air bubbles.
  5. Working in small batches, pipe 6 inch pieces of the dough into oil. Cook for 2-4 minutes on each side or until golden.
  6. Remove the churro from the oil and place onto a paper towel lined plate for about 30 seconds. Then coat the churro in the cinnamon sugar before placing into a serving bowl. Serve on its own or with hot Champurrado.
