Pickled Red Onions

Ingredients:

  • Red onions, thinly sliced, as needed
  • Salt brine, 2 tsp Salt per 1 cup Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Fill a jar with thinly sliced red onions. Then pour in the salt brine nearly to the top. Place a fermentation weight to ensure that the onions are completely submerged and then loosely cover.
  2. Place into a room temperature and dark location. Let ferment for about 7 days or to preference, checking occasionally that the onions are fully submerged. Once fermented to preference, store in the fridge or use immediately. Serve as a topping, in sandwiches, or as desired.
