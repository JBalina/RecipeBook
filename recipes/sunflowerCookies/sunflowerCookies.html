Sunflower Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Sunny Butter
  • 1/4 cup Maple syrup or Honey, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup Cornmeal

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Stir together the sunny butter and maple syrup/honey.
  3. Use a tablespoon to scoop balls of the dough and roll it in the cornmeal.
  4. Place the balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and press down on them slightly.
  5. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes or until firm.
