Agua Fresca

Ingredients:

  • 2-4 cups Fruit, Watermelon, Pineapple, Strawberries or any fruit, cubed
  • 1-2 cups Water
  • 1-2 Lime, juiced
  • Agave nectar, to taste
  • Optional: Mint

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients into a blender and blend until completely smooth.
  2. Optionally, strain out any fruit pulp for a smoother drink. Serve chilled with ice.
