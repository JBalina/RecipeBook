Chinese Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

Broth:

  • 6 cups Low sodium Chicken stock/broth
  • 4 cloves Garlic, smashed
  • 1 inch Ginger, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 4 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Sesame oil

Noodles and Toppings:

  • 8 oz Dried egg noodles
  • 1/2 lb Baby bok choy, halved
  • 2 cups Cooked shredded chicken breasts
  • 2 Scallions, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Combine the broth ingredients into a large pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Let simmer, covered, for 10 minutes.
  2. While the broth cooks, cook the dried noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Add in the bok choy to the broth to cook for 1 minute. You can optionally remove the ginger and garlic from the pot.
  4. Add in the noodles and chicken. Mix and let it return to a simmer. Then remove from heat and add in the scallions. Serve hot.
