Raita

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 Medium onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp Cilantro, chopped
  • Optional: 1-2 Green chilis, deseeded and finely chopped
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Cumin

Instructions:

  1. Into a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt and salt with a fork.
  2. Mix in the onion, cucumber, cilantro, and chili if using. Optionally, garnish with cumin.
