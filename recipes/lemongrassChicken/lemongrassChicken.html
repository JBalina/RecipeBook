Lemongrass Chicken

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 1 lb Chicken thighs, cut into 1" pieces
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/3 tsp MSG or Chicken bouillon

Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sambal
  • 1/2 cup Coconut juice
  • Lemongrass Chicken:
    • 4 tbsp Canola oil
    • 3 Shallots, minced
    • 1 tbsp Garlic, minced
    • 4-5 tbsp Lemongrass, minced
    • Optional: Scallions, minced for garnish
    • 1/2 Onion, very thinly sliced for garnish

    Instructions:

    1. Combine the marinade ingredients and let marinate for at least 15 minutes or overnight.
    2. Combine the sauce ingredients.
    3. Add the oil to a wok over medium-high heat. Add the shallots and sauté until light brown. Then add in and sauté the garlic until light brown. Then add in the lemongrass and sauté until fragrant.
    4. Add the chicken and sauté until browned. Lower the heat to medium and pour in the sauce. Stir to combine and then cover with a lid. Cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is completely cooked. Taste and adjust for seasoning.
    5. Remove the lid and add in the garnish. Toss for 1-2 minutes or until the garnish is just wilted/softened. Remove from the heat and serve hot with rice.
