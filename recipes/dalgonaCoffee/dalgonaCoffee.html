Dalgona Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Boiling water
  • Milk
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, add in the coffee, sugar, and water. Mix on high speed with an electric hand mixer until it forms stiff peaks.
  2. Top a glass of milk with ice and the whipped coffee.
