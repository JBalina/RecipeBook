Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Apple juice, freshly made
  • Optional: 1/4 cup Ginger bug
  • Optional: Cinnamon sticks

Instructions:

With Ginger Bug:

  1. Pour the apple juice and ginger bug into flip-top bottles. Optionally, add in 1/2 cinnamon stick per 16 oz bottle. Let ferment for 3-7 days or until carbonated to preference.

Without Ginger Bug:

  1. Pour the freshly made apple juice into a 1/2 gallon jar. Optionally, add in 2 cinnamon sticks for flavor. Cover with a cheesecloth and stir several times a day until it becomes bubbly.
  2. For stronger carbonation, transfer to flip-top bottles. If desired, add in 1/2 cinnamon stick per 16 oz bottle. Let ferment for 2-5 days or until carbonated to preference.
