Fruit Leather

Ingredients:

  • Any Fruit
  • Optional: Honey or Maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Blend the fruit in a blender until completely smooth. Place into a dehydrator in a thin layer, between 1/4" - 1/8". Let dehydrate for about 8 hours or until completely dry to the touch.
  2. Peel off of the dehydrator tray and roll into a log. Optionally cut the log into roughly 1 inch segments. Serve immediately or store for later.
