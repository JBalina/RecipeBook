Jägerschnitzel

Ingredients:

Schnitzel:

  • Schnitzel

Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 clove Garlic, minced
  • 16 oz Button mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 4 tbsp Flour
  • 2 cups Beef broth
  • 1-2 tsp Balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a pot or a large pan, heat the butter over medium high heat until melted. Add in the onions and cook until starting to brown. Then add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add the mushrooms and cook until soft and the water from the mushrooms evaporates, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the butter and melt. Then add the flour and stir constantly for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the beef broth, vinegar, salt, sugar, thyme, and pepper. Stir until the gravy is thickened. Lower the heat to low and cover. Cook for 5-10 minutes. If the gravy is too thick, add water. If it is too thin, cook until reduced.
  5. Pour the gravy over the schnitzel to serve.
