Sourdough Starter

Ingredients:

  • Water, nonchlorinated
  • Rye flour
  • Unbleached AP flour

Instructions:

  1. Into a 1 quart, loosely lidded jar, combine the flour and water as specified every day for at least 7 days to start the sourdough starter. From then onwards, continue to feed the starter for as long as you wish for it to be active.
    • Day 1:
      • 100g Rye flour
      • 150g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 2:
      • 70g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 115g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 3:
      • 70g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 115g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 4:
      • 70g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 100g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 5:
      • 70g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 100g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 6:
      • 50g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 100g Water, 85 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Day 7:
      • 25g Yesterday's starter
      • 50g Rye flour
      • 50g Unbleached AP flour
      • 100g Water, room temperature
  2. From day 7 onwards, repeat day 7's feedings. Feed daily. If wanting to take a break from feedings, refrigerate and switch to feeding weekly.
