Creamy Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Pasta
  • 1 lb Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Medium onion
  • 3 Carrots
  • 3 Celery stalks
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • 32 oz Low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp Parsley
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp Flour
  • 2 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1/3 cup Heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pasta.
  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrot and celery and saute until soft, about 3-4 minutes, then add garlic and saute 1 minute longer.
  3. Add chicken broth, parsley, bay leaves and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add chicken breasts and bring the soup to a boil. Reduce heat to medium, cover with lid, and allow the soup to simmer until the chicken has cooked through, about 10-15 minutes depending on the thickness of the breast.
  5. Remove the chicken and allow to rest for 5 minutes, then shred into small bite size pieces.
  6. In a separate pan over medium heat, melt the butter and add flour and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 1/2 minutes.
  7. While constantly whisking, slowly add in milk and whisk until there are no lumps.
  8. Whisk in the heavy cream and bring mixture to a boil while stirring constantly. Pour the milk mixture into soup mixture and return chicken to soup along with the cooked pasta and stir.
