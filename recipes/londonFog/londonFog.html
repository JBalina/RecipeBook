London Fog

Ingredients:

  • 1 Earl grey tea bag
  • 1/2 cup Boiling water
  • 1/2 cup Milk, hot
  • 1/4 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Honey or Sugar, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brew the tea in the hot water.
  2. Combine the milk and vanilla extract. Steam or froth the milk.
  3. Stir in the sweetener to the tea and then top with the milk. Serve hot or cold.
