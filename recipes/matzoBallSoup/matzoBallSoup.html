Matzo Ball Soup

Ingredients:

Soup:

  • 1 tbsp Canola Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Yellow onion, diced
  • 3 stalks Celery, diced
  • 3 Carrots, quartered and thinly sliced
  • 1 Chicken breast, cut in half if very large
  • 6 cups Chicken broth
  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 sprigs Dill
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp Dill, chopped

Matzo Balls:

  • 3/4 cup Matzo meal
  • 3 tbsp Schmaltz, or substitute canola oil
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 3 tbsp Water

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the matzo ball ingredients and mix until homogenous. Place into a refrigerator and let chill.
  2. Heat the canola oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saut√© the garlic, onion, celery, and carrots for 5 minutes or until the onions are transparent.
  3. Add in the chicken breast, chicken broth, water, sprigs of dill, and freshly cracked black pepper. Place a lid on the pot and bring to a boil. Once it reaches a boil, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30 minutes, covered.
  4. After the soup has simmered for 30 minutes, remove the chicken breast and let it rest for 1-2 minutes. Then shred it with a fork and return it to the soup. Taste the soup and add salt if needed.
  5. Form the matzo meal mixture into ping pong ball sized balls and carefully drop them into the soup. Let the soup simmer for 20 more minutes, covered.
  6. Remove the cover and taste and adjust salt and pepper to preference. Remove from heat and add in the chopped dill. Serve immediately.
