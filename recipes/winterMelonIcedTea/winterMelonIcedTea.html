Winter Melon Iced Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1000 g Winter melon, thinly sliced
  • 150 g Brown sugar
  • 50 g Rock sugar
  • 300 g Water + more as needed

Instructions:

  1. Toss the winter melon in a large bowl with the brown sugar. Cover with plastic wrap and let it sit for 1 hour for the winter melon to absorb the sugar and release liquid.
  2. Transfer the winter melon and brown sugar into a pot with the rock sugar and water. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Cover and let cook for 1 hour.
  3. Strain the contents of a pot through a sieve to remove all solids. Add cold water and ice as needed until sweetness is reduced to preference. Transfer to a fridge and serve cold over ice.
