Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 can Chickpeas or 1 1/2 cups Cooked chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup Tahini
  • 1/4 cup Lemon juice
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp Aquafaba (Chickpea water), or water
  • Optional: Paprika or Sumac, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients into a food processor and process for a few minutes until smooth. Add in more salt as needed for taste and more aquafaba as needed for a smoother hummus. Serve with bread, crackers, falafel, or whatever you like.
