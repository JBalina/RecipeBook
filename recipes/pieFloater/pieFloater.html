Pie Floater

Ingredients:

Meat Pies:

  • 1 batch Aussie Meat Pies

Pea Soup:

  • 1 lb Frozen peas
  • 1/2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1 small/medium Potato, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cups Chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp Mint, chopped
  • Salt, as needed
  • 1 tbsp Crème fraîche

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until translucent, 5-7 minutes. Then add the peas, potato, mint, and stock. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer over low. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until the potato is fork tender.
  2. Blend the soup together with an immersion blender. Optionally, continue to simmer the soup until it reaches a desired consistency. Remove from the heat. Taste and adjust salt as needed.
  3. Pour some of the soup into a bowl with the crème fraîche to temper it. Then whisk it back into the soup.
  4. Serve the soup with a pie placed into it upside down. Top with ketchup.
