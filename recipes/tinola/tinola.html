Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 3 lb Chicken legs and thighs
  • 1 tbsp Canola oil
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 3 inch Ginger, sliced
  • 3 tbsp Fish sauce
  • 5 cups Water
  • 1 Chayote, cut into wedges
  • 1/2 lb Spinach
  • 1 Bok Choy, chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat. Saut√© the onions, garlic, and ginger until the onions become translucent.
  2. Add chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 to 7 minutes or until chicken starts to change color.
  3. Add the fish sauce and cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Add the water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat to a simmer and let cook, covered, for 35 minutes.
  5. Add in the chayote and let cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add spinach and bok choy and cook until wilted.
