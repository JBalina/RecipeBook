Salsa Roja

Ingredients:

  • 3 Roma tomatoes, stem removed
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Jalape√±o, stem removed and optionally deseeded
  • 1/4 cup White onion, diced
  • 1/4 bunch Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tsp Olive oil
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Coarsely blend the tomato, garlic, and jalapeno in a blender or food processor.
  2. Heat a saucepan with the olive oil over medium high heat. Add the tomato, garlic, jalapeno mixture and let it cook until it reaches a boil.
  3. Put the salsa in a bowl, add the cilantro and onion, and stir well. Add salt to taste.
