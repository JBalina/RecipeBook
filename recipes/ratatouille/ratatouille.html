Ratatouille

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Tomatoes, woody cores removed
  • 1 medium Eggplant, about 1 lb, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 large Yellow squash, about 1/2 lb, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 medium Zucchini, about 1/2 lb, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 large Red, Orange, or Yellow Bell pepper, about 1/2 lb, diced into 1/2 inch squares
  • 6 tbsp Olive oil, divided
  • 3/4 tsp Salt, divided + more to taste
  • 1 medium Yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Basil, finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Either grate the tomatoes or place the tomatoes into a food processor and pulse until a smooth.
  3. In a large bowl, toss together the eggplant, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Distribute evenly onto one of the baking sheets. Then toss together the bell pepper, yellow squash, zucchini, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Distribute evenly onto the other baking sheet. Place the eggplants into the bottom rack of the oven and the other vegetables onto the top rack. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. While the vegetables bake, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Then add the diced onions. Sauté for about 8-10 minutes or until the onions just start to caramelize. Then add in the garlic and sauté for another minute or until fragrant. Then stir in the tomatoes, bring to a simmer, and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  5. Once the 15 minutes have passed, check on the vegetables in the oven. Toss the vegetables in the trays and switch the trays so that the eggplants are on the top rack and the other vegetables are on the lower rack. Let bake for 10 more minutes.
  6. After 10 minutes, remove the eggplants from the oven. Transfer the eggplants from the tray into the pot with the tomatoes. Stir and continue to let simmer while the other vegetables continue to bake for another 5-10 minutes, or until the start to caramelize.
  7. After 5-10 minutes and the other vegetables are caramelized, transfer them from their trays to the pot. Stir and let simmer for 5 more minutes.
  8. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the basil, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, and black pepper. Taste and add salt and pepper as desired. Serve immediately.
