Green Bean Gomaae

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb Green beans, trimmed and cut in 2 inch pieces
  • 3 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add in the green beans and let cook for about 5 minutes. Then strain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Toast the sesame seeds in a pan over medium-low heat for a couple minutes or until they are fragrant and you can hear a popping noise. Then grind them into a course paste.
  3. Combine the sesame seeds with the soy sauce and sugar. Then toss the green beans with the dressing until evenly coated. Serve at room temperature.
