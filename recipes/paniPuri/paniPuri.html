Pani Puri (Golgappa)

Ingredients:

Aloo Stuffing:

  1. 3 (630 g) Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  2. 1 small Red onion, diced
  3. 2 tbsp Cilantro, chopped
  4. 1/2 tsp Cumin
  5. 1/2 tsp Chaat masala
  6. 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  7. 1/2 tsp Salt
  8. 1/4 tsp Black pepper

Theeka Pani:

  • 1/4 cup Mint
  • 1/2 cup Cilantro
  • 1 inch Ginger, peeled
  • 2 Green chilies
  • 1 small Tamarind, peeled and deseeded
  • 4 cups Cold water
  • 1 tsp Chaat masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Asafoetida

Khatta Meetha:

  • 1 cup Tamarind water
  • 3 tbsp Jaggery or Sugar
  • 3 cups Cold water
  • 1 tsp Chaat masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp Asafoetida

To Serve:

  • Puri
  • 2 tbsp Boondi

Instructions:

  1. For the aloo stuffing, add the potatoes to a large pot of salted water and bring to a boil. Once boiling, let cook for about 15 minutes or until tender.
  2. Strain the water from the potatoes. Mash the potatoes into a serving bowl and then add in the other stuffing ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  3. For the theeka pani, add the mint, coriander, ginger, chilies, tamarind, and water as needed to a blender. Blend until very smooth. Then transfer to a large serving bowl. Add the other theeka pani ingredients and stir together to combine.
  4. For the khatta meetha, add the khatta meetha ingredients to a bowl and stir to combine.
  5. Add 1 tbsp boondi each to the theeka pani and khatta meetha. Serve all items. To consume, punch a hole into a puri with your thumb and fill with the aloo stuffing. Then dip into either the theeka pani or khatta meetha and consume. Alternatively, simply pour the theeka pani or khatta meetha over the stuffed pani and consume. Eat each puri in one bite.
