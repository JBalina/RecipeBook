Autumn Harvest Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Wild rice flour
  • 1/2 cup Fine cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup Sunny Butter
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Maple sugar or 3 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 1/4 cup Dried cranberries, Chopped walnuts, or Cooked wild rice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a bowl, combine the wild rice flour, cornmeal, and salt. Then mix in the sunny butter, maple sugar/honey, and oil to form a stiff dough.
  3. Form the dough into 1 inch balls and place onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, slightly flattened.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until slightly firm. Serve warm or cooled.
