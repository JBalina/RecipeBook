Simple Syrup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Lemon/Lime juice
  • Optional: 1 tsp Flavoring, Vanilla, Rose water, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Add the sugar, water, and lemon/lime juice to a pot and bring to a boil. Then reduce to low and let simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the flavoring.
  2. Let cool before using or transfer to the fridge to use later.
