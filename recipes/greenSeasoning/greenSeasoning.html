Green Seasoning

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch Culantro
  • 1/2 bunch Parsley
  • 2 stalks Celery or Chinese celery
  • 3 Scallions
  • 10 cloves Garlic
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 inch Ginger
  • 8 sprigs Thyme
  • 1 Scotch bonnet

Instructions:

  1. Place all the ingredients into a food processor. Pulse until mostly smooth or to preference. Use immediately or store in the fridge or freezer.
