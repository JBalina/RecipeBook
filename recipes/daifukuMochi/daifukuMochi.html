Daifuku Mochi

Ingredients:

  • Mochi, cut into flat circles
  • Red bean paste

Instructions:

  1. Optionally, freeze scoops of red bean paste for 30 minutes or until firm.
  2. Take a scoop of red bean paste, and place onto the center of a circle of mochi. Wrap the mochi around the red bean paste and press the edges of the dough together, squeezing to seal.
