Tahini Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup Tahini
  • 1/2 cup Lemon juice, about 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Optional: 1 cup Parsley, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Place all the ingredients except for the parlsey into a food processor. Process until homogenous, adding more water as needed for your desired consistency.
  2. Transfer to a serving bowl and mix in the parsley if using. Taste and add salt if needed. Serve with falafel or warm pita.
