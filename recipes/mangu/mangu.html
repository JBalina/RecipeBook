Mangú Con Los Tres Golpes

Ingredients:

Mangú:

  • 4 Green plantains
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4 tsp Olive oil

Quick Pickled Onions:

  • 2 Red Onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Red wine vinegar
  • Salt

Other Ingredients:

  • Queso de Freir, sliced
  • Salami, sliced
  • Eggs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

Mangú

  1. Peel the plantains. Cut each lengthwise and then in half.
  2. In a pot, cover plantains with water. Boil until very tender. Add salt right before the water just starts to really bubble.
  3. Once tender, drain the plantains and mash until smooth.
  4. Add in the water and olive oil and mix until thoroughly incorporated.

Quick Pickled Onions

  1. Slice the onions. Fry the onions in a pan with the olive oil over medium heat until translucent.
  2. Add in the red wine vinegar and salt and mix thoroughly.

Los Tres Golpes

  1. Prepare by cutting the salami and cheese into slices.
  2. Heat oil over medium heat. Begin frying and plating the salami, then the cheese, and then the eggs.
