Hot Cocoa

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Cocoa powder
  • 1 - 2 tbsp Sugar, or to taste
  • 1/16 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Milk
  • Optional: 1/4 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Mini-marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Combine the cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and about 2 tablespoons milk into a small saucepan over medium-low heat and whisk until the cocoa powder and sugar are completely dissolved.
  2. Slowly whisk in the rest of the milk and heat it over medium heat until it is hot. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla. Pour into a mug and serve topped with mini-marshmallows.
