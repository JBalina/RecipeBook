Salabat

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz Ginger, thinly sliced
  • 6 cups Water
  • 1/4 cup Honey
  • 1/2 Calamansi or Lemon, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the ginger, water, and honey into a pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Cover and let simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Add in calamansi to taste. Serve hot.
