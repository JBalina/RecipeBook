Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Oatmeal
  • 1 cup Ice
  • 1 cup Milk (preferably almond milk)
  • 2 tbsp Peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp Cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Add the oatmeal to the blender and blend until they become a fine powder.
  2. Add the ice, milk, peanut butter, cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla extract and blend until smooth.
