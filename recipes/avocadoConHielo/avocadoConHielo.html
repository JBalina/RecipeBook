Avocado con Hielo

Ingredients:

  • 1 Avocado, mashed
  • 1 cup Milk or Evaporated milk
  • 2 tbsp Condensed milk, or to taste
  • 1 cup Ice, crushed

Instructions:

  1. Stir together the milk and condensed milk until homogenous.
  2. Place the mashed avocado into the bottom of a serving glass. Add the crushed ice and then pour the evaporated over the ice.
  3. Serve cold. Stir before consuming.
