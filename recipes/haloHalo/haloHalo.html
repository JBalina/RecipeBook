Halo-Halo

Ingredients:

Halo-Halo:

  • 1 scoop Ube ice cream
  • Shaved ice
  • Milk, Evaporated milk, and/or Condensed milk
  • Halo-halo mix

Halo-Halo Mix Options:

  • Ripe banana
  • Macapuno
  • Firm gelatin
  • Sweet beans
  • Leche flan
  • Jackfruit
  • Tapioca pearls

Instructions:

  1. Layer the Halo-halo mix into a serving cup. Then top with shaved ice and pour over the milk. Top with a scoop of ube ice cream. Serve immediately.
