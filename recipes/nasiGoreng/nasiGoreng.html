Nasi Goreng

Ingredients:

  • 1/2~ lb Chicken
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Canola oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1-2 Bird's eye chili, minced
  • 1 small Onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp Kecap manis, divided
  • 3 cups Cooked rice
  • 2 tsp Belacan
  • 1 Scallion, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok over high heat. Add in the chili and garlic and stir-fry until fragrant, about 10-30 seconds. Then add the onions and stir-fry for about another minute. Add the chicken and stir-fry until mostly cooked. Then add in 1 tbsp kecap manis and stir-fry about 1 more minute.
  2. Add in the rice, the rest of the kecap manis, and the belacan. Stir-fry, tossing very often, for about 2 minutes or until the sauce is reduced and evenly distributed. The rice should begin to caramelise.
  3. Remove from heat and toss in the scallion. Serve immediately.
