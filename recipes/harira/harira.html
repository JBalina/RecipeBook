Harira

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1 Carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 stalks Celery, and finely chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tbsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne, or to taste
  • 3 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 28 oz can Crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup Green lentils or Brown lentils
  • 1 cup Red lentils
  • 1 14oz can Chickpeas
  • 1 bunch Cilantro, chopped
  • 7 cups Low sodium Vegetable or Chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup Rice or Broken vermicelli
  • Optional: Lemon wedges
  • Optional: Harissa paste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add in the onions, carrot, and celery and season with salt to taste. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until softened.
  2. Add in the garlic and ground spices. Sauté for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and cilantro. Add some more salt to taste and sauté for about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the stock and bring to a boil. Let boil for about 5 minutes before covering and reducing the heat to low. Cook over low for about 45 minutes, occasionally adding more water/stock if thickening too much.
  5. Stir in the rice and then cook, covered, for another 15 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately, optionally with lemon wedges and some harissa paste.
