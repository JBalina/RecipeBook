Refried Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 Onion or 1 small Onion
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 15oz can Black beans drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Dice the onion. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan on medium heat and fry the onion with the salt until softened and turning translucent.
  2. Once softened, mix in the cumin while making sure to stir constantly until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  3. Then, add the drained beans and water. Stir and then cover and let cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and remove the cover. Use a potato masher or a fork to mash the beans to your preference. Stir and let cook for 2-3 more minutes or until desired thickness.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in pepper and optional lime juice. If too thick or dry, stir in a splash of water.
