Braised Sunchokes

Ingredients:

  • 1 - 1/2 lb Sunchokes, cut in half lengthwise
  • 2 - 3 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 1/4 Wild onions or Shallots, chopped
  • 2 tsp Sage, chopped
  • 1/8 tsp Smoked salt
  • 1/4 cup Corn stock, Vegetable stock or Water
  • Optional: 1/4 cup Roasted sunflower seeds
  • Optional: Wojape

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add in the onion, sage, and salt and sauté until the onions become translucent.
  2. Add in the sunchokes, flat-side down and sauté for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add in the stock or water and reduce the heat to low. Cover and let cook for 40 minutes.
  4. Serve warm and garnish with sunflower seeds and/or a drizzle of wojape.
