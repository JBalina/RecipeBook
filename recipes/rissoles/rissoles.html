Rissoles

Ingredients:

Rissoles:

  • 1 lb Ground beef, about 90/10
  • 1 small Onion, grated
  • 1/2 Panko bread crumbs or Bread crumbs, or more as needed
  • 1 small Carrot, peeled and grated
  • 1 small Zucchini, Grated
  • 1 large clove Garlic, minced
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

To Fry:

  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions:

  1. Combine the grated onion and breadcrumbs. Then mix in the rest of the rissole ingredients until well combined.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Form the meat mixture into 1/4 cup sized thick patties. Add patties in batches of 4-5 to the skillet. Cook for about 4 minutes per side and then transfer to a serving plate. Repeat for the rest of the meat mixture.
  3. Serve immediately with ketchup.
