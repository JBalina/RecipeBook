Fig Jam

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Figs, stemmed
  • 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 Lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup Water

Instructions:

  1. Combine all of the ingredients into a blender. Blend to preference.
  2. Transfer to a sauce pan over medium-low heat. Whisk constantly while cooking for 20-35 minutes or until thickened to preference, around when the jam runs off the side of a spoon in thick, heavy drops rather than as a stream.
  3. Either can or store in a fridge. Serve with toast.
