Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 8-12 oz Egg noodles
  • 1 lb Top sirloin, cut into strips
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 8 oz Brown mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 1 cup Beef broth
  • 3/4 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup Sour cream
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 2 Green onion greens, minced
