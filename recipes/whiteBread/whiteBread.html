White Bread

Ingredients:

  • 284 - 340 g Water
  • 32 g Honey
  • 2 1/4 tsp Instant yeast
  • 11 g Salt
  • 28 g Butter, softened
  • 480 g Flour
  • 37 g Milk powder

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients into the bowl of a stand mixer. Mix on the second lowest setting until it forms a smooth dough
  2. Place the dough into a greased sealable container and cover. Let rise for 60-90 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  3. Deflate the dough and flatten into a rectangle. Then roll it into a 9" log. Place it into a greased 9x5 loaf pan, cover, and let rise for another 60-90 minutes, or until it has crowned 1 - 1 1/2" over the rim of the pan.
  4. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  5. Bake the bread for 20 minutes. Then tent it with foil and bake another 15-20 minutes or until the internal temperature reads 195-200 degrees Fahrenheit.
  6. Remove the bread from the oven and transfer from the loaf pan onto a wire rack. Let cool completely before slicing and serving.
