Sourdough Popovers

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (113g) Sourdough starter
  • 1 cup (227g) Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup (120g) Flour

Instructions:

  1. Place a muffin tray into an oven and preheat to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine the sourdough starter, milk, eggs, and salt into large bowl. Mix with a handmixer on low. Do not whip the mixture. Then slowly mix in the flour. It should be about the consistency of heavy cream.
  3. Remove the muffin tray from the oven and carefully grease it with non-stick spray. Fill in every other muffin cup all the way with the mixture. Spacing the popovers will give them room to expand without touching.
  4. Place the muffin tray back into the oven and bake at 450 degrees Fahrenheit for 15 minutes. Then reduce the heat and cook for 15-20 more minutes, or until evenly golden. Remove from the oven and serve immediately.
