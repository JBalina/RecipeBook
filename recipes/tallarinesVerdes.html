Tallarines Verdes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Dried spaghetti
  • 5 oz Baby spinach
  • 1 cup (3/4 oz) Basil
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Shallot
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts
  • 4 oz Queso fresco
  • 1/8 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, reserving 1/4 cup pasta water.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to a blender and blend until very smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  3. Add the spaghetti and the sauce to a large bowl and toss to combine. Add some reserved pasta water as needed for consistency. Serve hot with steak or chicken.
