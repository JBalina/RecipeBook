Camarones Guisados

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Yellow onion, diced
  • 1 Green bell pepper, diced
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup Pimento stuffed olives
  • 1 28 oz can Tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/4 bunch Cilantro
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp Black pepper, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add in the onions and bell pepper and sauté until the onions are translucent, about 5-7 minutes. Then add in the garlic and sauté another minute.
  2. Add in the tomatoes, olives, water, cilantro, bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer over medium. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed. Then add in the shrimp and cook to preference, about 5-15 minutes. Then remove from heat and serve immediately.
