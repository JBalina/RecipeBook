Cevapi

Ingredients:

  • 1-1.3 lb Ground beef, 85/15
  • 0.5-0.6 lb Ground Pork
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • Optional: 3/4 tsp Baking soda

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients. Let marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour or preferably overnight.
  2. Shape the meat mixture into small sausages, about the length and width of an index finger.
  3. Prepare either a grill or a skillet with some olive oil. Cook the sausages for about 4-5 minutes per side or until golden.
  4. Serve the cevapi immediately with lepinja, ajvar, and raw onions.
