Damper

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (240 g) Flour
  • 1 tbsp Baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 20 g Butter, cubed
  • ~1 cup Water, or half Water, half Milk, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven or dutch oven to 360 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients and combine evenly. Then add in the butter and incorporate it well with your hands. Then stir in the water with a rubber spatula until it forms a dough. Do not overknead.
  3. Dust the dough with flour and transfer onto a parchment paper. Flatten lightly and then place into the oven/dutch oven for about 25 minutes or more. If using a dutch oven, optionally remove the lid towards the end to add some color. The damper is done when it sounds hollow when tapped on.
  4. Remove from the oven and let cool completely. Slice and serve.
