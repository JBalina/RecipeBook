Red Corn with Cotija

Ingredients:

  • 2 ears Red corn, kernals only
  • 2 tsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Lime juice
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Cotija, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp Cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the corn kernals and sugar. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Sauté for another 1-2 minutes. Remove from the heat and toss with the cotija and cilantro.
