Filipino Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 4 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 lb Hotdogs or Ground beef (preferably Filipino hotdogs)
  • Pasta sauce
  • 4 slices American Cheese
  • 4 oz Condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and cook the spaghetti noodles until they are ready.
  2. Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Fry the onion in the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Once the onion becomes translucent, add in the minced garlic. Fry for about a minute or until fragrant.
  4. Add in the hotdogs or ground beef and fry until browned completely.
  5. Add the pasta sauce and stir until heated through.
  6. Mix in the cheese, condensed milk, and honey. Stir until completely incorporated and heated through.
