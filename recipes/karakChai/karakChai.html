Karak Chai

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Loose leaf black tea or 3 tea bags, or more to taste
  • 2 Green cardamom, crushed
  • 1/8 tsp Saffron threads
  • Optional: 2 Cloves
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Evaporated milk

Instructions:

  1. Place the water into a saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Add in the tea and let boil for 1 minute.
  2. Add in the cardamom, saffron, and cloves. Let boil for another 30 seconds.
  3. Mix in the sugar and milk. Let come to a foaming boil and then remove from heat to allow the foaming to stop. Place back onto heat and repeat for 5 minutes or until the tea is a caramel color.
  4. Strain and serve immediately.
