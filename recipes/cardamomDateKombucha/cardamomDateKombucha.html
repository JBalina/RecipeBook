Cardamom Date Kombucha

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombucha, after 1st fermentation
  • 2-4 Dates, minced
  • 2 Green cardamom, lightly crushed

Instructions:

  1. Evenly divide the dates into 2 16 oz flip-top bottles. Then add 1 crushed cardamom to each bottle. Fill up the remaining space with the kombucha. Let ferment for 3-7 days, or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
