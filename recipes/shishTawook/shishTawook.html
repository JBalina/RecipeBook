Shish Tawook

Ingredients:

Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 lb Chicken breast, cut into 1 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1/2 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Lemon juice, about 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil
  • 6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 3/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Ground ginger
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper

Other Ingredients:

  • Optional: 1 Onion, cut in half and then quartered (8 pieces)

Instructions:

  1. Combine the marinade ingredients together and let marinate overnight.
  2. Thread the chicken onto the skewers right before grilling. Add a piece of onion to the middle of each skewer if using.
  3. Grill on medium heat for 5-8 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve on its own or with pita bread.
