Wild Rice Pilaf

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Cooked wild rice
  • 2 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 1 lb Fresh wild mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Wild onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Sage, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Corn or Vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup Dried cranberries
  • 1 cup Roasted chestnuts, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup
  • 1/2 - 2 tsp Smoked salt, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add in the mushrooms, onions, and sage and saut√© until the mushrooms and onions are softened and browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in the wild rice, stock, and cranberries and cook until the stock is mostly evaporated.
  3. Stir in the maple syrup, chestnuts, and salt. Remove from heat and serve.
