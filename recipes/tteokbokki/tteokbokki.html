Tteokbokki

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Cylinder rice cakes
  • 4 cups Water
  • 8 large Sardines, about 2 cans
  • 6x8 inch Dried kombu, 1 sheet
  • 1/3 cup Gochujang
  • 1 tbsp Gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 3 Scallions, cut into 3 inch pieces
  • 2 Hard boiled eggs
  • 1/2 lb Fish cakes

Instructions:

  1. Place the sardines, kombu, and water into a shallow pan. Bring to a boil and then cook over medium-high heat for 15 minutes.
  2. Strain the sardines and kombu from the broth. Mix in the rest of the ingredients and continue to cook for 10-15 minutes, continously stirring gently with a wooden spoon. Serve hot.
