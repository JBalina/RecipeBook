Pão de Queijo

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Tapioca flour
  • 1 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 6 tbsp Canola oil
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Mozzarella cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine the milk, water, oil and salt in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium high heat.
  3. Once the milk mixture has come to a boil, remove from heat and mix in the flour.
  4. Once the mixture has cooled for a little bit, mix in the eggs. It will appear like it will not mix at first, but after a mixing for a while it will come together.
  5. Once the eggs are mixed in, begin to mix in the cheese, a little at a time, until fully incorporated. If the mixture is too liquidy, add more flour. If it is too thick, add more milk.
  6. Use wet hands and a spoon to scoop the dough and form into uniform small balls.
  7. Place the balls on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper and place it into a preheatd oven. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until they are golden and puffed. Serve warm.
