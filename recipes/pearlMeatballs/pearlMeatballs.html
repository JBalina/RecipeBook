Zhēn Zhū Wán Zi (Pearl Meatballs)

Ingredients:

Meatballs:

  • 1 lb Ground pork, preferably 80% lean 20% fat
  • 1/3 cup Water chestnuts, minced
  • 2 tsp Ginger, minced
  • 3 tbsp Scallion whites, minced, Optionally reserve greens for garnish
  • 3 tbsp Cold water
  • 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 tsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp White pepper

Other Ingredients:

  • 8 Dried bamboo leaves
  • 3/4 cup Glutinous rice
  • Optional: Scallion greens for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak the bamboo in boiling water the night before cooking.
  2. Soak the rice water for at least 2 hours before cooking. Then drain the rice.
  3. Combine the meatball ingredients into a bowl. Mix in one direction for about 5 minutes until the mixture is fluffy and sticky. Cover and place into fridge to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Prepare the steamer. Begin to boil the water and line the steamer trays with the bamboo leaves.
  5. Take a tablespoon of the meat mixture and roll it into a ball. Then roll it in the rice until the surface is completely coated. Roll it one last time in your hands to make sure the rice sticks to the meat. Place into the steamer trays about 1/2 inch apart.
  6. Steam for about 20 minutes and then remove from heat. Let rest for 3 more minutes without removing the cover.
  7. Garnish with scallion greens if desired and serve.
