Filipino Barbecue

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 4 lb Pork shoulder, sliced into thin pieces
  • 3/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup Banana ketchup
  • 1/2 cup Calamansi or Lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup Sprite or any Lemon lime soda

Basting Sauce:

  • 3/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup Banana ketchup
  • 1/2 cup Calamansi or Lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients and let marinade overnight.
  2. Heat up a grill. Combine the marinade ingredients together. Place the marinated meat onto skewers.
  3. Grill the skewers for about 3-5 minutes per side or until completely cooked. Baste each skewer when flipping. Serve immediately with some spicy vinegar.
