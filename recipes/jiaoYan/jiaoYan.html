Jiao Yan

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp White pepper
  • 2 tbsp Salt

Instructions:

  1. Toast the Sichuan peppercorns over medium-high heat for about 30 seconds.
  2. Grind the peppercorns in a spice grinder. If there is too much space in the grinder, add the white pepper to the grinder as well.
  3. Mix together the ground Sichuan peppercorns, white pepper and salt together.
