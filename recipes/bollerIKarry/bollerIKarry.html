Boller i Karry

Ingredients:

Meatballs:

  • 1 lb Ground pork
  • 1 Onion, finely diced
  • 1 large Egg
  • 4 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 2 cups Chicken stock

Curry:

  • 1/3 cup Butter
  • 2 tbsp Curry powder
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • 1 cup Chicken stock, from the meatballs
  • 1 cup Milk, or more to preference
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Espelette pepper or Cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine the ground pork, onion, egg, flour, salt, and pepper and form into 1 tbsp sized meatballs.
  2. Place the 2 cups chicken stock into a large saucepan and bring to a boil. Add in the meatballs and then reduce to a low simmer. Cover and let cook for about 8 minutes.
  3. Melt the butter over medium-low heat in a large pan. Whisk in the curry powder and tumeric and let cook for 30 seconds. Then whisk in the flour until homogenous.
  4. Whisk constantly while slowly pouring in the chicken stock. Then do the same with the milk. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Also stir in espelette or cayenne if using.
  5. Add the meatballs into the sauce. Stir to coat the meatballs and let cook until the meatballs are heated through. Serve immediately with rice or bulgur.
