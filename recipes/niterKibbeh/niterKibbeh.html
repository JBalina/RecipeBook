Nit’ir Qibe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup Onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 3 Black cardamom pods
  • 1 2-inch stick Cinnamon
  • 3 Cloves
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp Black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp Coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp Ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp Ground turmeric
  • Optional: 1 tbsp Besobela
  • Optional: 1 tbsp Kosseret

Instructions:

  1. Place just the whole spices into a saucepan over medium heat. Toast for 1-3 minutes or until very fragrant. Be careful not to burn.
  2. Once fragrant, add in all the other ingredients. Bring to a simmer over very low heat. Simmer over low heat for 60 - 90 minutes.
  3. Pour the butter through a cheesecloth and into a jar. Let cool completely before sealing.
