Kielbasa and Sauerkraut

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Kielbasa, sliced diagonally
  • 2 tbsp Canola oil
  • 6 medium/small Red potatoes, cubed
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 lb Sauerkraut, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add in the potatoes and sauté until lightly browned, about 7-11 minutes.
  2. Add in the onions and continue to sauté until translucent, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add in the kielbasa, sauerkraut, and pepper. Sauté for 4-5 minutes or until heated though. Serve hot.
