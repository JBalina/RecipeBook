Rose of Sharon Cheong

Ingredients:

  • 1 part in grams Rose of sharon petals
  • 1 part in grams Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Ensure that the rose of sharons are well cleaned and then dry to the touch.
  2. Layer the sugar and petals into a sterilized glass jar. Cover loosely and let ferment in a room temperature and dark environment for about 1 week or to preference.
