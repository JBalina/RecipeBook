Melon

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cantaloupe
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 4-6 cups Water

Instructions:

  1. Cut the cantaloupe in half and scoop out the seeds with a spoon
  2. Using a coconut grater, shred the flesh of the cantaloupe into a pitcher.
  3. Mix in the water and the sugar. Serve chilled or over ice.
