Ful Mudammas

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Dried fava beans + 5 cups Water, OR 2 14 oz cans Fava beans
  • 1/2 Water
  • 1-2 Mild peppers, like jalape√±os, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 1 Lemon, juiced
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • Salt, to taste
  • Olive oil, to taste
  • 1 cup Parsley, chopped
  • 1 Tomato, diced

Instructions:

  1. If using dried beans, rinse the beans and then place in a bowl covered with water. Let soak the night prior. Then place into a pot with 5 cups water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat to a simmer and cook, covered, for about 1-2 hours, or until done and tender. Strain from the water and reserve the beans.
  2. In a mortar and pestle, add in the garlic and pepper. Mash into a paste. Then mix in the lemon. Reserve.
  3. Into a 10" cast iron skillet over medium heat, add in the beans and 1/2 water. Mash the beans to preference. Add the cumin and salt to taste.
  4. Pour the garlic pepper paste over the beans. Then drizzle with olive oil to taste. Top with the parsley and then with the tomato. Serve immediately with warm pita and/or vegetables.
