Puerto Rican Sofrito

Ingredients:

  • 3 Green Bell peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 10 Ají dulce, stemmed and seeded
  • 3 White onions, peeled and quartered
  • 1 head Garlic, peeled
  • 1 Bunch Cilantro, stems included
  • 15 leaves Culantro

Instructions:

  1. Combine all of the ingredients into a large food processor. Purée until finely minced. Transfer to jar and place into a fridge, or use immediately.
