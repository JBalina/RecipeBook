Haldi Dooh (Golden Milk)

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups Milk
  • 3/4 tsp Ground turmeric
  • 4 Green cardamom, slightly crushed
  • 1/2 inch Ginger, thinly sliced
  • 6 Whole black peppercorns
  • 1 inch Cinnamon, slightly crushed
  • 1 tbsp Honey, or preferred sweetener

Instructions:

  1. Add milk to a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to right before a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  2. Whisk in the turmeric. Then add in the rest of the ingredients. Let simmer over low for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and cover. Let steep for 5 more minutes. Strain into cups and serve hot.
