Horchata

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Uncooked long-grain rice
  • 5 cups Water
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tbsp Ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup White sugar

Instructions:

  1. Put the rice and water into a blender and blend until the rice begins to break down, 1 minute. Let stand in room temperature overnight.
  2. Strain the rice water into a pitcher and discard the rice. Stir the milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and sugar into the rice water. Chill and stir before serving over ice.
