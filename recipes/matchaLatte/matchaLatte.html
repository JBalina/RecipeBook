Matcha Latte

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp Matcha powder
  • 2 tsp Sugar, or to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp Hot water
  • 1 cup Milk, cold or hot to preference
  • Optional: Ice

Instructions:

Hot Latte:

  1. In a mug, mix the water and matcha power with a whisk to ensure there are no lumps.
  2. Warm the milk in a saucepan and pour it into the mug. Mix with a whisk until smooth.
  3. Mix in the sugar to taste.

Iced Latte:

  1. Optionally, in a small bowl, whisk together the matcha and water.
  2. Then in a blender bottle, combine all the ingredients and ice. Shake vigorously until completely combined.
