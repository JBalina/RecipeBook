Baleadas

Ingredients:

Tortillas:

  • 420-480 g Flour
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Canola oil
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Filling:

  • 2 cups Refried beans, preferably with Pinto beans
  • Crema fresca
  • Queso fresco, crumbled
  • Optional: 1-2 Avocado, sliced
  • Optional: 4 Eggs, scrambled

Instructions:

  1. Combine together the tortilla ingredients into a mixing bowl and knead until smooth and no longer sticky, starting with 420 grams flour and adding more as necessary. Divide the dough into 8 even balls and cover the mixing bowl with a wet towel. Let rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Stretch the dough balls out into thick tortillas, about 8-10 inches in diameter each. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook each tortilla until browned and lightly puffed, about 1 minute per side.
  3. Layer refried beans, avocado, queso fresco, and any other fillings over tortillas. Drizzle the crema fresca on top. Fold tortillas in half over filling. Serve immediately.
