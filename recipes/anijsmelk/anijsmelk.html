Anijsmelk

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Anise seeds
  • 1-2 tsp Sugar or Honey

Instructions

  1. Optionally, lightly crush the anise seeds with a mortar and pestle.
  2. Place the milk and the anise seeds into a small sauce pan. Heat over medium low until it reaches a gentle simmer.
  3. Mix in the sweetener and reduce the heat to very low. Let gently simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the seeds from the milk and serve.
