Wild Bergamot Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

Wild Bergamot Sugar:

  • 1 - 1 1/2 cup Monarda fistulosa tips (leaves and tender stems)
  • 3/4 cup White sugar

Cookies:

  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups Unsalted butter, cubed and at room temperature
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups Flour
  • 1 1/4 cups Cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/4 cup Powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. For the bergamot sugar, grind the two ingredients together coarsely in a food processor, until the sugar turns green and there are no more visible plant pieces.
  2. In a very large bowl, cream together the butter, bergamot sugar, and brown sugar. Then, beat in the eggs.
  3. Into a separate large bowl, sift the flour and cocoa powder. Add the baking soda and mix to combine.
  4. Gradually and slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, until well incorporated.
  5. Seal the dough with saran wrap and let chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
  6. Form the dough into 1 1/2-inch balls with your hands. Place the dough balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, about 1 inch apart.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 5 minutes. Then rotate the tray and bake for another 3-5 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and sift the powdered sugar over the cookies to gently dust.
