Peanut Butter & Jelly

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices Challah, or any bread
  • Any Nut butter
  • Any Jelly, Jam, or Preserves

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of bread in either a toaster or in an oven.
  2. Slather the nut butter on one slice of bread, and the jam on the other. Combine the two slices and serve.
