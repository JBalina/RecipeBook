Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Strawberries, preferably frozen
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Milk
  • Optional: 1 cup Ice, if not using frozen strawberries
  • Optional: 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • Optional: Sugar, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve cold.
