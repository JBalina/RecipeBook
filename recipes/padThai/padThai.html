Pad Thai

Ingredients:

Sauce:

  • 1 1/2 tbsp Tamarind paste
  • 3 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Oyster sauce

Other Ingredients:

  • 4 oz Dried rice stick noodles, 2-3mm/0.1 inch thick
  • 1/2 lb Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 small Yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups Beansprouts
  • 1/2 cup Firm tofu, cut into batons/julienned
  • 1/4 cup Garlic chives , cut into 3 cm / 1 1/4 inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup Peanuts, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Place noodles in a large bowl and pour over boiling water until covered. Soak for 5 minutes. Then, drain in a colander and quickly rinse under cold water. Do not leave sitting for more than 5 - 10 minutes.
  2. Mix Sauce in small bowl.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large wok over high heat. Add garlic and onion and cook for 30 seconds. Add chicken and cook for 1 1/2 minutes until mostly cooked through.
  4. Push the chicken to one side of the pan and pour the egg in on the other side. Scramble the eggs and add more oil if too dry, then mix the eggs into chicken.
  5. Add the bean sprouts, tofu, and noodles. Then add in the sauce. Toss gently for about 1 1/2 minutes, until the sauce is absorbed by the noodles.
  6. Add the garlic chives and half of the peanuts. Toss and mix thoroughly and quickly, then remove from heat.
  7. Serve immediately, sprinkled with remaining peanuts and lime wedges on the side.
