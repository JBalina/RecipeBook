Chakalaka

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Red onion, diced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 inch Ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp Curry powder
  • 1 Red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 Green bell pepper, diced
  • 4 medium-large Carrots, grated or julienned
  • 1 14 oz can Diced tomatoes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp Red chili flakes
  • 1 15 oz can Baked beans in tomato sauce

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until translucent, 5-7 minutes. Then add in the garlic, ginger, and curry powder and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add in the carrots and sauté for about another 1-2 minutes. Then add the tomatoes and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook, covered for 5-10 minutes or until the mixture has thickened.
  3. Stir in the baked beans, chili flakes, salt, and pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed. Serve immediately.
