Ají Amarillo Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 Ají amarillo, or 2 tbsp Ají amarillo paste
  • 1/3 cup Queso fresco
  • 1/4 Canola oil
  • 1 large clove Garlic
  • 3 tbsp Lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp Huacatay paste
  • 1/8 tsp Sugar
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients into a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. Serve as a dipping sauce.
