Tuscan Gnocchi

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Uncooked potato gnocchi
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 1 Shallot, sliced
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 8 oz jar Dried tomatoes in oil, removed from oil + 2 tsp Reserved oil
  • 1/2 cup Chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Dried Italian herbs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cup Baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup Grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp Basil, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Melt the butter and sauté the shallots until transparent, about 2 minutes. Sauté garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Add the potato gnocchi and let sear for 1 minute. Then add the dried tomatoes and oil. Mix and let sear for another minute.
  3. Mix in the chicken broth, heavy cream, and italian herbs, scraping any browned bits from the pan. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and let cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove cover and stir in the spinach. Cook until wilted, about 1 minute.
  5. Mix in the parmesan and basil and let cook for 1 minute or until the gnocchi is soft and the sauce is reduced to preference.
