Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Milk
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Heavy cream
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Oreos, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a pot over medium heat, add in the milk and sugar. Heat until the sugar completely dissolves, then remove from heat.
  2. Add in the heavy cream and vanilla extract. Transfer the mixture to a fridge and let chill until very cold.
  3. Pour the mixture into an already churning ice cream maker. Let churn until soft serve consistency, 20-30 minutes.
  4. Add in the chopped oreos and let churn for 5-10 more minutes. Transfer the mixture to a sealable container and place into the freezer until firm. Serve cold.
