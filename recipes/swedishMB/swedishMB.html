Swedish Meatballs

Ingredients:

Meatballs:

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 lb Ground pork
  • 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 Egg yolks
  • 1/4 tsp Allspice
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Gravy:

  • 1/4 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • 4 cups Beef broth
  • 3/4 cup Sour cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Parsley, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Finely dice the onion. Heat half of the olive oil over medium heat in a pan and fry the onion until translucent, about 3 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, Panko breadcrumbs, egg yolks, allspice, nutmeg and cooked onion. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Stir until well combined. Roll the mixture into 1 1/4-to-1 1/2-inch meatballs
  3. Heat the other half of the olive oil in the pan over medium heat. In batches, fry the meatballs until all sides are browned. Reserve the meatballs.
  4. To make the gravy, melt butter in the same skillet with any fat from frying the meatballs. Whisk in flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute.
  5. Gradually whisk in beef broth and cook, whisking constantly until slightly thickened for about 1-2 minutes.
  6. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Stir in meatballs and cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through and thickened, about 8-10 minutes.
  8. Remove from the heat and thoroughly mix in the parsley
