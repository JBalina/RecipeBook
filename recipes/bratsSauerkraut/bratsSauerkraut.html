Brats with Sauerkraut

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 Bratwursts
  • 14 oz Sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
  • 2 Apples, finely diced
  • 3 strips Bacon, cooked and diced
  • 1 small Onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Ground mustard powder
  • 6-8 Hotdog buns

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sauerkraut, apples, bacon, onion, sugar, and mustard. Place the bratwursts into a slow cooker and then scoop the sauerkraut mixture over it. Set the slow cooker to low and cook for 6-8 hours, or until the internal temperature of the bratwursts is at least 160 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Once the bratwursts are done, place them into hotdog buns with some of the sauerkraut mixture. Serve hot.
