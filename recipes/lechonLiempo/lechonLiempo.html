Lechon Liempo

Ingredients:

Rub:

  • 4 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 head Garlic
  • 1 small Onion
  • 2 Lemongrass, tender part only
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp White vinegar
  • 2 tsp Black pepper

Stuffing:

  • 2 Scallions
  • 1/2 head Garlic
  • 3-4 Shallots
  • 4 Lemongrass, tough outer parts removed

Lechon Liempo:

  • 5 lb Full pork belly
  • Soy sauce, to taste
  • Olive oil, as needed
  • Milk, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Combine the rub ingredients into a food processor and pulse until mostly smooth.
  2. Shallowly pierce the skin side of the pork belly with a sharp knife or fork all over. Score the flesh side diagonally about every 1-2 inches in both directions to make a diamond pattern. Massage the rub into only the flesh side. Place the stuffing ingredients over the flesh side of the pork belly. Roll the pork belly as tightly as possible over the stuffing and tie with butcher's twine about every inch. Pat the skin dry. Rub a coat of soy sauce over the skin, followed by a coat of olive oil.
  3. Place the pork belly into a rotisserie and let cook for 90-120 minutes or until fully cooked and the internal temperature reads at least 145 degrees Fahrenheit. Baste every 10-15 minutes with olive oil or stock/broth or pan drippings for the first hour, followed by milk for the remaining time.
  4. Remove from the rotisserie. Slice and serve the lechon with lechon sauce.
