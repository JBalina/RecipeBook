Tahini

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Sesame seeds
  • 4-8 tbsp Olive oil, Sesame oil, or any neutral flavored oil

Instructions:

  1. Toast the sesame seeds in a large pan for a few minutes, until lightly browned and fragrant. Then remove from heat and allow to cool.
  2. Transfer the sesame seeds to a food processor. Process until very crumbly and it starts to release its oils, about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add in oil as needed and continue to process until it forms a smooth paste, about another 3-5 minutes. Use immediately or store in a jar in the fridge.
