Corn Nuts

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups large Dried hominy, soaked overnight in water
  • 4 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp Maple sugar
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Sumac

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Dry the hominy with a towel until dry to the touch. Then toss the hominy in a large bowl with the oil. Sprinkle with the seasonings and lay evenly onto a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 25-35 minutes, shaking often, until darkened and crispy.
  4. Remove and serve immediately.
