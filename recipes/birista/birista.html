Birista

Ingredients:

  • 2 Onions, finely sliced
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Heat up about an inch of oil in a large wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and allow them to fry until browned and crispy. Stir occasionally to make sure they're frying evenly.
  3. Remove the onions from the oil and place onto a paper towel lined plate.
