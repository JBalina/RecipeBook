Wild Rice Flour

Ingredients:

  • Uncooked wild rice

Instructions:

  1. Place the wild rice into a food processor and blend until small beads.
  2. Transfer from the food processor into a coffee grinder and grind until a fine powder.
  3. Sift the flour through a sieve to filter out any missed larger pieces. Place the larger pieces back into the coffee grinder and repeat until a fine powder.
