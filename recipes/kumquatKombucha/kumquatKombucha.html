Kumquat Kombucha

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombucha, after 1st fermentation
  • 16 Kumquats, halved and then thinly sliced

Instructions:

  1. Evenly divide the kumquats into 2 16 oz flip-top bottles. Then fill up the remaining space with the kombucha. Let ferment for 3-7 days, or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
