Crock Pot Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Chicken breasts
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/8 tsp Dried rosemary
  • 1/8 tsp Dried thyme
  • 3 cups Chicken broth
  • 1 large Russet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup Frozen corn
  • 1 cup Frozen peas
  • 1 cup Frozen carrots
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • Optional: 3 tbsp Cornstarch + 1/4 cup Water
  • Optional: Fresh parsley, for garnish
  • 8 Biscuits

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts to taste with salt and pepper. Place the chicken, onion, garlic, herbs, chicken broth, potato, and frozen vegetables into the slow cooker. Set the slow cooker to high and let cook for 3 hours.
  2. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it with forks. Place it back into the slow cooker. Whisk together the milk and flour and mix it into the slow cooker. If using, mix the cornstarch slurry into the slow cooker for a thicker soup. Let cook for another hour.
  3. Mix in chopped parsley before serving. Serve with a biscuit.
