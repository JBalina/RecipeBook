Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients:

  • 4 1/2 lb Chicken legs or thighs, bone-in, skin-on
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 8 cloves Garlic, smashed
  • 1 Leek, halved and then sliced
  • 1 lb Oyster mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 Carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 3-4 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1 1/4 cup Red wine or White wine
  • 1 28oz can Lightly crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp Fresh oregano, 1 sprig Rosemary, and/or 5-10 sprigs Thyme
  • Optional: 1 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 lb Wild mushrooms, or about 1 1/2 oz of Dried wild mushrooms reconstituted
  • 1/2 - 1 cup Kalamata olives, whole
  • 1/3 bunch Parlsey, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or a very large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add in the garlic and sear on both sides until golden.
  2. Add in the leek with a little salt and sauté for about 10 minutes, or until softened. Then add in the oyster mushrooms and carrots and sauté for another 7-10 minutes, or until the mushrooms are softened.
  3. Add in the chicken and coat with the rest of the salt. Toss the contents of the pan together until mixed. Then pour in the red wine. Bring to a simmer and let reduce for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss in the tomatoes, bell pepper, bay leaves, fresh herbs, and red pepper flakes. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer over low. Cover and let simmer for 50 minutes.
  5. Then remove the lid and toss in the wild mushrooms. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer over low again. Cook for another 20 minutes, uncovered, or until the sauce has reduced to preference.
  6. Taste and add salt as needed. Garnish with olives and parsley and serve immediately.
