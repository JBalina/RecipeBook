Cacık

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Yogurt
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4 Persian cucumbers
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tbsp Dried mint
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Grate the cucumbers with a box grater. Then transfer to a cheese cloth and squeeze out as much liquid as you can.
  2. Whisk together all the other ingredients until very smooth. Then stir in the grated cucumbers. Taste and add salt if needed.
  3. Transfer to a fridge and let chill for 30 minutes or more to thicken. Serve cold.
