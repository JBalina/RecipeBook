Harissa Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 lb Chicken thighs (about 8 thighs), boneless, skinless
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 1 head Garlic, finely minced
  • 1/2 Red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tbsp Ground coriander
  • 1/2 tbsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne, or to taste
  • 5-6 tbsp Harissa paste
  • 1 Lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all of the ingredients and let marinate overnight.
  2. Heat a grill over medium/medium-high heat. Grill for 6-7 minutes on each side or until an internal temperature reads 165 degrees Fahrenheit. Serve hot.
