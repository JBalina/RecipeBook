Egusi

Ingredients:

Meat and Broth:

  • 1 lb any Meat, preferably Beef tripe + Stew beef
  • 4-5 cups Water, or as needed
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 Red onion, diced

Tomato Sauce Blend:

  • 3 medium Tomatoes, stemmed
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Red onion
  • 1 Scotch bonnet chili, optionally deseeded

Egusi:

  • 1/2 cup Palm oil OR 1/4 cup Palm oil + 1/4 cup Vegetable oil
  • 1/2 Red onion, diced
  • 1/3 cup Ground crayfish/shrimp
  • 1 cup Ground egusi
  • 1 cup Spinach, or to preference

Instructions:

  1. Place the meat and the broth ingredients into a large pot. Bring to a boil, and then reduce to low. Let simmer, uncovered, until tender, about 1 hour. Then strain out the meat and reserve both the broth and the meat.
  2. Meanwhile, place the tomatoes, garlic, onion, and pepper into a blender. Blend until smooth.
  3. Once the meat and broth are ready, heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add in the onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Then add in the meat and the ground crayfish. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato blend and 1-2 cups of the broth. Bring to a boil and then reduce back to medium. Let simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the ground egusi and then let simmer for 10 more minutes. Stir occasionally and add broth as desired for consistency.
  6. Add in the spinach. Let simmer for 5 more minutes. Serve hot with fufu.
