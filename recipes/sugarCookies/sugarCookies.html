Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Butter, softened
  • 1 1/3 cup Sugar, 283 g
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cup Flour, 355 g
  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Optional: Additional sugar or sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream the butter and the sugar in a large bowl with an electric hand mixer. Then add in the egg and vanilla extract and beat again.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Then add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until homogenous.
  4. Scoop the dough into about 2 tbsp size balls and roll in the additional sugar or sprinkles if using. Then place onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet a few inches apart and slightly flatten.
  5. Bake for 11-13 minutes or until slightly cracked on top.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving.
