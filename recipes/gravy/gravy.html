Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 1 Beef bouillon cube
  • 1 Chicken bouillon cube
  • 2 1/4 cups Water, or Low sodium stock
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 4 tbsp Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp Ground black pepper
  • Salt, if needed

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil and mix in the bouillon cubes. Let dissolve completely.
  2. In a separate saucepan, add the butter and flour and whisk together until homogenous over low heat. Optionally, continue to whisk over low heat until the roux darkens to a preferred color.
  3. Slowly add in the water/stock while whisking constantly. Then whisk in the onion powder and black pepper. Taste and add salt if needed. Serve over meat or biscuits.
