Sweet Lassi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Milk, and/or Ice
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp Cardamom
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Rose water
  • Optional: 1/16 tsp Saffron

Instructions:

  1. If using saffron, mix it with 1-2 tablespoons of hot milk while preparing the other ingredients.
  2. Blend all the ingredients together. Either blend in the saffron or use as a garnish.
