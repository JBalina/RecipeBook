Flavored Yogurt

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Honey, or to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 tsp Vanilla extract, or any other flavoring, to taste
  • Optional: Fresh berries

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the yogurt, honey, and flavoring until homogenous. Optionally top with berries before serving.
