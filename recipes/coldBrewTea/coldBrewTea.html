Cold Brew Tea

Ingredients:

  • 8 cups Water
  • 8 tsp Black tea, Green tea, or any Tea
  • Optional: Mint
  • Optional: Lemon
  • 1/3 cup Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Combine the water, tea, and mint and place into a large jar. Place into the fridge and let brew for 12 hours for green tea or at least 24 hours for black tea.
  2. Strain the tea into a pitcher. Stir in the sugar and lemon juice. Serve cold.
