Borscht (Red Beet Soup)

Ingredients:

  • 3 Medium beets, peeled and grated
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 8 cups Chicken broth
  • 2 cups Water
  • 3 Medium gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 Carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 Celery ribs, diced
  • 1 Red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp Tomato sauce or substitute 4 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1/2 head Cabbage, shredded
  • 1 14.5 oz can White cannelini beans
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 2-3 tbsp White vinegar, or to taste
  • 1 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper, freshly ground
  • 1 clove Garlic, finely minced
  • 3 tbsp Dill, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil over medium to high heat. Add in the grated beets and sauté for 10 minutes or until soft.
  2. Add in the broth, water, potatoes and carrots. Let cook for about 15 minutes or until fork tender
  3. While the potatoes and carrots are cooking, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil over medium to high heat in a pan. Add in the onion, bell pepper, and celery. Sauté for 7-8 minutes. Then add in the tomato sauce and stir-fry for 30 seconds. Transfer the contents of the pan to the soup pot along with the shredded cabbage to finish cooking with the potatoes.
  4. When the potatoes are done cooking, add in the beans, bay leaves, salt, pepper, vinegar, garlic, and dill. Let cook for 2-3 more minutes and then taste and adjust for seasoning. Serve immediately.
