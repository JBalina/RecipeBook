Sesame Ginger Tofu Poke Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Tamarind
  • 1 1/2 Hot/Warm water

Instructions:

  1. Clean the tamarind. Add to the water and let rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Mash the tamarind in the water. Let rest another 15 minutes and then mash a final time.
  3. Strain the mixture to a strainer and reserve the liquid. Store in a fridge for about a month.
