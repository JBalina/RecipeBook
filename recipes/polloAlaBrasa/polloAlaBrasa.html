Pollo a la Brasa

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 lb Whole chicken
  • 4 cloves Garlic, grated
  • 1 tbsp Ají amarillo paste
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Ají panca paste
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1 tsp Coriander
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 Lime, juiced
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients together and massage it into all parts of the chicken. Let marinate overnight, occasionally flipping the chicken and re-rubbing the marinade into it.
  2. Truss and skewer the chicken. Place into a rotisserie and cook for 15 minutes per pound, about 60-75 minutes.
  3. Remove from the rotisserie and let cool for about 10 minutes. Carve and serve hot with ají amarillo sauce.
