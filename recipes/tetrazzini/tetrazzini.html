Tetrazzini

Ingredients:

Tetrazzini:

  • 1 lb Thin spaghetti or Linguine
  • 2 cups Cooked shredded chicken breasts or Cooked shredded Turkey
  • 8 oz Button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 Unsalted butter
  • 1 cup Mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Bread crumbs

Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • 2 cups Chicken broth or Turkey broth
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/4 cup Parsley, chopped
  • 1 cup Mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan, grated

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to box instructions until al dente. The rinse with cold water and reserve.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. To a large pot over medium heat, add in the butter. Sauté the mushrooms until they start to soften and turn golden. Then add in the onions and sauté for 5 more minutes. Add in the Garlic and sauté for one more minute. Remove everything from the pot and reserve.
  4. Place the pot back over medium heat. Add in the butter and continuously whisk in the flour. Continue to whisk for one minute.
  5. Begin to slowly whisk in the broth. Do this in small batches to minimize lumps. Season with salt and pepper. Then whisk in the milk and heavy cream. Let thicken for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Fold in the onion mushroom mixture, the chicken/turkey, pasta, and parsley into the pot. Then slowly mix in the mozzarella and parmesan.
  7. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Flatten the pasta and then top with the mozzarella mixed with the bread crumbs. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbling and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for 15-20 minutes before serving.
