Ají Verde Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 2 Jalapeños or Serranos, deseeded to preference
  • 2 bunches Cilantro
  • 1 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Scallion
  • 1/4 cup Queso fresco
  • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Ají Amarillo paste
  • 2 tsp Huacatay paste
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. Serve with pollo a la brasa, or any chicken.
