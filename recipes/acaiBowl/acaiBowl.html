Açaí Bowl

Ingredients:

Açaí Bowl:

  • 2 packets Frozen açaí purée
  • 1/2 Banana
  • 1/4 cup Milk, or favorite fruit juice
  • Optional: 1/2 cup Any fruit, preferably frozen

Optional Toppings:

  • Bananas
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Blackberries
  • Kiwis
  • Granola
  • Coconut shavings
  • Any other fruit

Instructions:

  1. Blend together the frozen acai, banana, milk, and frozen fruit.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a bowl. Top with desired toppings. Serve immediately.
