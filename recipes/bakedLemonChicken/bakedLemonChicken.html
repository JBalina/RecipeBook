Baked Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 Chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 1/3 cup Chicken broth
  • 4 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tsp Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper, or to taste
  • Optional: Fresh rosemary
  • Optional: Lemon, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Heat the butter in a large pan over medium-high. Brown the chicken breasts for about 2-3 minutes per side. Then remove from the pan and place into a baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together the chicken broth, lemon juice, honey, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the sauce over the chicken in the baking dish. Bake for 20-30 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reads 165 degrees Fahrenheit, making sure to baste the chicken every 5-6 minutes.
  5. Garnish with lemon and rosemary if desired and serve.
