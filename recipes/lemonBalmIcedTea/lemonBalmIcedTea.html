Lemon Balm Iced Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Lemon balm, pounded
  • 2 quarts Water or 1 quart Water + 1 quart Ice
  • 1/3 cup Sugar or Honey

Instructions:

  1. Place 1 quart of the water into a pot and bring to a boil. Then remove the pot from the heat and add in the lemon balm. Cover and let steep for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in the sugar or honey. Then stir in the ice or rest of the water. Let chill in the fridge and then serve cold over ice.
