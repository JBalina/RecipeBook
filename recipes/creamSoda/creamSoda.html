Cream Soda

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Water, non-chlorinated
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 4 tsp Black tea, ~3g, or 4 tea bags
  • 1/4 cup Raisins
  • 4 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup Ginger bug

Instructions:

  1. Place the water, and sugar into a pot. Heat until boiling and then remove from heat. Add in the raisins and tea and let steep until cooled completely.
  2. Stir in the ginger bug and vanilla extract. Strain into flip top bottles and let ferment for 3-7 days, burping regularly. Serve cold over ice.
