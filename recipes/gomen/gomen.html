Gomen

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch Collard greens, stems removed, roughly chopped
  • 4 tbsp Nit’ir Qibe
  • 1 Onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp Ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp Ground coriander
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons nit’ir qibe over medium to medium-high heat. Add in the onion and sauté for 5-7 minutes or until soft and translucent.
  2. Add in the garlic, ginger, and spices. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the collard greens and another tablespoon of nit’ir qibe and cook until the collards turn bright green and are wilted but still a slightly crispy, 5-7 minutes.
