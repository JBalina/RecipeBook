Rose Jelly

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Rose syrup
  • 5 g Agar agar
  • Optional: Red or Pink food coloring

Instructions:

  1. Combine the ingredients into a pot and bring to a boil while whisking constantly. Let simmer while whisking for about 2 minutes or until the agar agar has just about fully dissolved. If the rose syrup does not already contain food coloring, food coloring may be added for a nicer color.
  2. Remove from the heat and transfer to a square steel tray. Let set at room temperature or in the fridge. Cube and serve chilled.
