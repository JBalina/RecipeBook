Sega Wat

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Beef chuck, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 6 tbsp Nit’ir Qibe
  • 3 cups Onion, pureed
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup Berbere spice blend
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Beef broth
  • 4 Hard-boiled eggs, pierced with a fork all over

Instructions:

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons of nit’ir qibe in a large pot. Add in the onion puree and cook, covered, over low heat for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Stir in the garlic, ginger, and 1 more tablespoon of nit’ir qibe. Cook, covered, over low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the berbere and the rest of the nit’ir qibe and cook, covered, over low heat for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mix in the beef, broth, and salt. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Cover, and simmer for 45-60 minutes, or until the beef is tender, stirring occasionally. Then taste and adjust for seasonings.
  5. Add in the eggs and simmer for 10 more minutes. Serve immediately
