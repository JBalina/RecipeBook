Sunchoke Soup

Ingredients:

Soup:

  • 2 lb Sunchokes, roughly chopped
  • 1 Leek, rinsed and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Wild onions, chopped
  • 2-4 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 5 cups Water or Vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp Salt

Optional Fried Sage Garnish:

  • Sage leaves
  • 1 tbsp Sunflower oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add in the leek and onion and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add in the sunchokes, water/broth, and salt. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Let cook covered for 30 minutesor until the sunchokes are tender.
  3. Remove the pot from heat and blend using an immersion blender until completely smooth. Taste and add salt in desired.
  4. For the fried sage, heat the oil in a small pan over medium heat. Add the sage leaves and sauté until crispy, less then 2 minutes.
  5. Serve and garnish with the sage leaves if desired.
