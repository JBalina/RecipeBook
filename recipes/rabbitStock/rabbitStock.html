Rabbit Stock

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 Rabbit carcass
  • 12 cups Water
  • 1 Onion or 3-4 Wild onions, halved, root-end cut off, skin kept on
  • 1 sprig Cedar or 1 tbsp Dried cedar
  • 5-6 Juniper berries
  • 3-4 sprigs Sage
  • Optional: Any other vegetables
  • 3-4 sprigs Mint
  • Optional: Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the rabbit and water into a large pot. Bring the water to a boil and then reduce to a simmer. Let simmer for 30 minutes while removing and impurities that float to the surface.
  2. Add in the onion, cedar, juniper berries, and sage. Add any other optional vegetables if desired. Let simmer for another 2 1/2 hours.
  3. Add in the mint. Let cook for another 30-45 minutes.
  4. Add salt to taste if desired. Let cool and then strain into containers.
