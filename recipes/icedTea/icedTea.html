Iced Tea

Ingredients:

  • 8 tsp Black tea, or 8 tea bags
  • 4 cups Water
  • 4 cups ice
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1-2 Lemons

Instructions:

  1. Bring the water to a boil. Then add in the tea leaves. Let steep for 10 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar until dissolved.
  3. Add in the ice and stir until chilled completely. Strain into a pitcher. Add more water if needed until it reaches 8 cups of iced tea.
  4. Add lemon to taste and stir. Serve cold.
