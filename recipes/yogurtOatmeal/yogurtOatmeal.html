Overnight Oatmeal Yogurt

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup Oatmeal + 1 tsp for garnish
  • 1/3 cup Milk
  • 1/3 cup Yogurt
  • Optional: 1 tsp Chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup Fruits or Berries of choice

Instructions:

  1. Into a 16 oz cup or jar, combine all the ingredients except for the berries until homogenous. Then fold in the berries. Garnish with 1 teaspoon oatmeal and seal with lid or plastic wrap. Let rest in the fridge overnight or up to 5 nights. Stir one last time right before eating.
