Patatnik

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp Butter, divided, 2 tbsp diced
  • 4 tbsp Flour, divided
  • 3 lb Potatoes, peeled and grated
  • 2 medium Onions, grated
  • 4 Eggs
  • Optional: 130 g Feta, crumbled
  • 2 tsp Dried mint
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Coarse black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter and melt it in a 12 inch cast iron skillet. Remove from heat. Tilt the skillet to have the butter coat all surfaces evenly. Then take 2 tbsp flour and dust the skillet to evenly coat all the sides.
  3. Place the potatoes into a towel lined bowl and squeeze out most of the liquid, about 1/2 - 2/3 cup liquid. Then transfer to a mixing bowl with the onions, eggs, feta, mint, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly to combine.
  4. Transfer the mixture to the cast iron skillet. Spread evenly. Then top as evenly as possible with the diced butter. Top again with a dusting of the rest of the flour.
  5. Place into the oven and bake for about 45 minutes, or until golden.
  6. Remove from the oven and let rest for at least 5 minutes. Serve hot.
