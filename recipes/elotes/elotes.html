Elotes

Ingredients:

  • Corn, husked, stalks left on
  • Mexican crema fresca
  • Cotija cheese
  • Chipotle chili powder and/or Ancho chili powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat a grill to medium heat. Grill the corn until completely cooked on all sides, about 8 minutes overall.
  2. Brush the crema fresca all over the corn. Then coat with the cotija cheese. Lightly powder with chili powder and serve.
