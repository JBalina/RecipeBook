Blatjang

Ingredients:

  • 680 g Dried peaches
  • 238 lb Dried apricots
  • 3 liters Brown wine vinegar, or any fruity vinegar
  • 500 g Red onion
  • 2500 g Sugar
  • 12 g Salt
  • 7 g Cayenne pepper

Instructions:

  1. Soak the peaches and apricots in the vinegar overnight, at least 12 hours.
  2. Remove the fruit from the vinegar and reserve the vinegar. Blend the fruit in a food processor and reserve. Blend the onion in a food precessor and reserve.
  3. Combine all the ingredients into a large pot and bring to a boil. Then reduce to a simmer and cook for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bottle immediately. It is ready to serve now, but will be better after maturing for 2 - 4 weeks.
