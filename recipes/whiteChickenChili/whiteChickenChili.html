White Chicken Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lb Chicken breast
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 15 oz cans Great northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 5 oz can Mild green chilis
  • 1 cup Salsa verde
  • 1 cup Chicken stock
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp Salt
  • 4 tbsp Cream cheese

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients except for the cream cheese into a slow cooker. Let cook for 4-5 hours on high or 6-8 hours on low.
  2. Remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it with forks.
  3. Using and immersion blender, blend the chili for 5-10 seconds to blend only some of the beans.
  4. Place the cream cheese into a separate bowl with a few spoons of the chili and stir until well incorporated. Then stir it back into the chili along with the shredded chicken.
  5. Serve hot with crumbled tortilla chips.
