Taco (Tacobell-style)

Ingredients:

Meat:

  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 tsp Chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Toppings:

  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Cheddar cheese, grated
  • Optional: Salsa or Hot sauce

Other:

  • Hard taco shells or Soft flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the toppings by shredding the lettuce, dicing the tomatoes, and grating the cheddar cheese.
  2. Over medium heat, fry the ground beef with the spices and the salt and pepper to taste.
  3. If using soft tortillas, toast them on the stove over medium to medium-low heat.
  4. Fill your tortilla or taco shell in the order of: ground beef, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, cheddar cheese, and optionally salsa or hot sauce.
