Roasted Figs

Ingredients:

  • 5 Fresh figs, stems removed and quartered
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Fresh rosemary
  • Honey, to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place the fig slices onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet. Combine the brown sugar and the cinnamon and sprinkle over the figs. Then, toss the rosemary over the figs.
  3. Bake in the oven for 40 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes. Drizzle over with honey.
