Pollo Guisado

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 lb Chicken thighs or legs (about 5 pieces), bone-in, skin-on
  • 1 tsp Adobo seasoning
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 large Potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup Chicken broth, or water
  • 1 8 oz can Tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup Recaito
  • 1/4 cup Pimento olives
  • 1 tbsp Achiote oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 Culantro leaf
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • Optional: 1/2 bunch Cilantro, to garnish

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken with the adobo seasoning. Heat the olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the chicken and brown them, about 5 minutes each side.
  2. Add in the potato, chicken broth, tomato sauce, recaito, pimento olives, achiote oil, cumin, italian seasoning, culantro leaf, and bay leaf. Stir together until well mixed and the chicken is coated in the sauce. Let it come to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook, covered, for 35 minutes or untilt the chicken is cooked through.
  3. Remove from heat. Optionally remove the culantro and bay leaf from the pot. Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.
