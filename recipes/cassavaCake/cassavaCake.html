Cassava Cake

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • 2 packs Grated Cassava (about 2 lbs)
  • 2 cups Coconut milk
  • 6 oz Evaporated milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 6 tbsp Grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup Condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup + 2 tbsp White sugar (14 tbsp)

Topping:

  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp Grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups Coconut milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. For the cake batter, combine the grated cassava, butter, condensed milk, evaporated milk, cheddar cheese, sugar, and the 2 whole eggs in a mixing bowl and mix thoroughly.
  3. Mix in the coconut milk while stirring.
  4. Grease a baking tray and pour the batter in. Place into the preheated oven and bake for 1 hour.
  5. For the topping, begin to heat a saucepan on low heat and mix in the sugar and flour.
  6. Pour in the condensed milk and stir thoroughly.
  7. While constantly stirring, add in the grated cheese.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk and stir constantly for 10 minutes or until your desired consistency.
  9. Pour the topping over the cake and spread evenly.
  10. Separate the last egg and with a basting brush use the whites to glaze the topping of the cake.
  11. Preheat the broiler and broil for a couple of minutes until the color turns a light brown.
  12. Garnish with grated cheddar cheese.
