Hibiscus Soda

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Water, nonchlorinated water
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 4 tsp Dried hibiscus
  • 2 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup Ginger bug

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients except for the ginger bug into a pot over medium heat. Bring to a boil and then remove from heat. Let steep until cooled completely to room temperature.
  2. Add in the ginger bug. Strain and pour into flip-top bottles to ferment for 3-7 days, burping regularly. Serve over ice.
