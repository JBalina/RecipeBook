Taro Bubble Tea

Ingredients:

  • 600 g Taro, peeled and cubed
  • 3 tbsp Sugar, or to taste
  • 2 cups Water
  • 4 tsp Black tea leaves, or 4 Black tea bags
  • 4 tsp Condensed milk
  • 4 cups Milk, or as desired for consistency
  • 1 1/2 cups Prepared tapioca pearls

Instructions:

  1. Place the taro into a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to medium. Let cook for 20 minutes, or until easily pierced with a fork.
  2. While the taro cooks, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Remove from heat and add the black tea. Let steep for 10 minutes and then strain out the tea leaves.
  3. Once the taro is finished, drain out the water from the pot. Using a food processor or an immersion blender, blend together the taro and sugar into a fine paste.
  4. Add in the black tea, condensed milk, and milk to the taro paste. Mix well or use an immersion blender. Taste and add more milk or sugar as desired.
  5. Serve cold with the prepared tapoioca pearls and ice.
