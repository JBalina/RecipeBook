Chocolate Covered Strawberries

Ingredients:

  • 10 oz Chocolate
  • 2 lb Strawberries

Instructions:

  1. Wash and completely dry the strawberries. Prepare a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Place the chocolate into a prepared double-boiler. Melt the chocolate while whisking until smooth.
  3. Holding the strawberries by the stems, dip the strawberries into the chocolate, coating them evenly. Then place carefully onto the baking sheet to cool.
  4. Place into the fridge for about 15 minutes to chill and for the chocolate to solidify.
