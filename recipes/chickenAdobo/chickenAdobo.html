Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 1 1/2 lb Chicken thighs, boneless and skinless (about five thighs)
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp White vinegar
  • 3 Bay leaves

For Cooking:

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Whole black peppercorns
  • Optional: 2 Scallion greens, minced

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken and marinade ingredients. Marinate overnight.
  2. Heat half the oil in a pan over high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade while reserving the marinade and sear for about one minute on each side. Do not cook chicken all the way through. Remove and reserve the chicken and its juices.
  3. Heat the remaining oil in the pan and saut√© the onion and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add the reserved marinade, water, sugar and black pepper. Bring it to a simmer, then turn heat down to medium high. Let simmer for five minutes.
  5. Add the chicken smooth side down. Simmer uncovered for about 15 minutes without stirring. Then flip the chicken and continue to simmer without stirring for 5-10 more minutes or until the sauce reduces down to a thick jam-like syrup. If the sauce is still not thick after 10 minutes, remove the chicken and continue to simmer until the desired thickness is achieved before placing back the chicken.
  6. Coat the chicken in the sauce and optionally garnish with the scallions.
