Hibiscus Iced Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Dried hibiscus
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 8 cups Water or 4 cups Water + 4 cups Ice
  • Optional: 2 inches Ginger, sliced
  • Optional: 1 tsp Allspice berries
  • Optional: 4 cloves
  • Optional: 1 Orange peel
  • Optional: 1/2 stick Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Add the sugar, water, and other flavorings to a pot and heat over high. Stir to dissolve the sugar while the water comes to a boil. Then remove from the heat and add the hibiscus. Cover and let steep for 20 minutes.
  2. Strain the mixture into a pitcher and add the remaining water or ice and stir to combine. Serve cold over ice, optionally with some fresh lime.
