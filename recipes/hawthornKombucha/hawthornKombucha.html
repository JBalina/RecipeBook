Hawthorn Kombucha

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombucha, after 1st fermentation
  • 60 g Dried hawthorn
  • 2 tbsp Honey, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the dried hawthorn into a food processor and pulse until chopped finely. Distribute the hawthorn and honey evenly between two 16 oz flip-top bottles. Fill up the remaining space with the kombucha. Let ferment for 3-7 days, or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
