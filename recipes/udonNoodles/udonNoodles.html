Udon Noodles Stir-fry

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 1 lb Meat of choice
  • 2-3 tbsp Soy sauce
  • Splash of rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda

Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Mirin

Other Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Udon Noodles
  • 1 large Onion
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 3 inch piece Ginger
  • Any Vegetables
  • 1 tbsp Dark soy sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Slice meat of choice into strips. Mix marinade ingredients and let the meat marinate overnight.
  2. Prepare sauce by mixing sauce ingredients together. Cut onion into slices and mince the garlic and ginger.
  3. Once the meat is ready to be cooked, fry until almost completely cooked and then remove from pan and reserve for later.
  4. In the same pan, begin to fry the onions, garlic, and ginger. Once softened, mix in the sauce.
  5. Add in the cooked noodles, meat, and vegetables. Stir until completely heated through.
  6. Optionally, mix in the dark soy sauce for a deeper color and flavor.
