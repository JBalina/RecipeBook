Dill Pickles

Ingredients:

  • 8 Pickling cucumbers, ends removed
  • 2 1/4 cup (532 ml) Water, nonchlorinated + 1 tbsp (18 g) Salt, to make a 3.5% brine + more if needed
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Optional: 1 inch Horseradish, peeled and sliced
  • 2 Dill umbrellas or 3-5 sprigs Dill
  • 12 Black peppercorns or Mixed peppercorns
  • 8 Coriander seeds
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 10 Mustard seeds
  • 2 Cloves
  • 2 berries Allspice

Instructions:

  1. Add in half the seasonings to the bottom of a half gallon jar. Then add in the cucumbers, organizing the top cucumbers to ensure nothing floats above the brine. Then add in the rest of the seasonings.
  2. Add in the brine to fully submerge the contents of the jar, leaving about 1/2" of headspace. Loosely cover and let ferment at room temperature for 3-4 days for half-sour pickles or 8-10 days for full-sour pickles.
  3. Taste and once ready, serve or transfer to a fridge to stop further fermentation.
