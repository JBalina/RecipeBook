Yogurt

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Milk
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt starter or Yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Make sure all the equipment is sterilized. Preheat an oven to 110 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Scald the milk. Bring the milk to 180 degrees Fahrenheit and then remove from heat.
  3. Let the milk cool. Once the milk comes down to about 110 degrees Fahrenheit, mix in the yogurt. Then place the milk into the oven and let ferment for about 8 hours before bottling and placing into a fridge.
  4. Optionally, after removing from the oven, let the yogurt sit for a few hours before placing into the fridge.
  5. For Greek yogurt, strain the yogurt with a cloth or towel until 1/4 cup of whey per cup of yogurt is strained out, about 30-60 minutes.
