Peach Ginger Kombucha

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombucha, after 1st fermentation
  • 1 Peach, peeled and pitted, pureed or finely minced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, minced

Instructions:

  1. Evenly distribute the peach and ginger into two 16 oz flip-top bottles. Fill the remaining space in the bottles with kombucha. Let ferment for 3-7 days or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
