Mandarin Orange Kombucha

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombucha, after 1st fermentation
  • 2-4 Mandarin oranges, peeled and crushed

Instructions:

  1. Mildly crush the mandarins and distribute them evenly into 2 16oz flip-top bottles. Fill the remaining space in the bottles with kombucha. Let ferment for 3-7 days or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
