Stuffed French Toast

Ingredients:

Whipped Cream Cheese:

  • 8 oz Cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup Powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla extract

Batter:

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Optional: 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Optional: 1 tsp Cinnamon

French Toast:

  • 6 thick slices Brioche, about 1 inch thick slices
  • Butter, for frying
  • Optional: Powdered sugar
  • Optional: Maple syrup
  • Optional: Fresh fruit

Instructions:

  1. With an electric hand mixer, whip together the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Then transfer the mixture into a piping bag with a long puff nozzle tip.
  2. Cut a cavity into the brioche slices with a pairing knife. Be careful not to pierce all the way through the bread. Use the piping bag to fill the bread slices with the whipped cream cheese mixture.
  3. Whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon into a shallow bowl.
  4. Heat 2 tablespoons butter in a pan over medium/medium-low. Dip both sides of a stuffed slice of brioche into the egg mixture until completely coated. Then transfer it to the pan and cook until both sides are golden brown, 2-5 minutes per side. Repeat for all the bread slices, adding more butter to the pan as needed. Serve hot, dusted with powdered sugar, and with maple syrup and fresh fruit.
