Fig and Goat Cheese Toast

Ingredients:

  • Crusty bread slices
  • Fig jam
  • Crumbled goat cheese
  • Optional: Figs, sliced
  • Optional: Balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices. Then top with a layer of fig jam, followed by a layer of goat cheese. Optionally top with some fig slices and/or a drizzle of balsamic vinegar. Serve immediately.
