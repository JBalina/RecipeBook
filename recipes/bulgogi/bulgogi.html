Bulgogi

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 6 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp Water
  • 4 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Rice wine or Mirin
  • 2 tbsp Garlic, minced
  • 4 tbsp grated Asian pear
  • 2 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 tsp Sesame seeds
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper

Other Ingredients:

  • 2 lb Thinly sliced beef (rib eye or top sirloin)
  • 3 Scallions, cut diagonally into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 small Onion, thinly sliced
  • Optional: 1 small Carrot, thinly sliced

Instructions:

  1. Combine and mix the marinade ingredients. Then, add the other ingredients and toss gently to combine. Let marinate overnight.
  2. Cook by either grilling, pan-searing, or stir-frying. If grilling or pan-searing, cook until slightly caramelized and make sure not to overcrowd. If stir-frying, it is okay to overcrowd the pan and add all the leftover marinade and let the meat cook in its juices.
