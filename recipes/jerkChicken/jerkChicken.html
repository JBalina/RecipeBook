Jerk Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 10 pieces Chicken Thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 6 Scallions, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 4 cloves Garlic, smashed
  • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers, or substitute 2 Habanero peppers
  • 1 1/2 inch piece Ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1/3 cup Lime juice, about 3 Limes
  • 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Allspice
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Place chicken pieces in a gallon size resealable bag. Add remaining ingredients to a food processor and pulse several times until it is well blended and somewhat coarse. Pour mixture over the chicken in the bag and seal the bag while pressing out excess air. Then rub marinade over chicken. Let rest in refrigerator 3 - 24 hours. Optionally, rub the marinade under the chicken skin for more flavor.
  2. Oven: Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with non-stick cooking spray. Remove chicken from marinade. Arrange pieces on baking sheet leaving some space between them. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 45-50 minutes. Optionally, broil during the last few minutes for better browning.
    OR
    Grill: preheat a gas grill to medium-high heat, about 400 degrees. Clean grill grates and rub lightly with oil using tongs and an oiled paper towel. Grill until chicken is cooked through, turning occasionally for about 30 minutes.
