Cranberry Ginger Ale

Ingredients:

Optional Spices:

  • 3 whole Cloves
  • 1 stick Cinnamon
  • 3 Allspice berries
  • Zest of 1 Orange or Lemon

Cranberry Ginger Ale:

  • 2 cups Water, nonchlorinated
  • 2 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 6 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups Cranberry juice
  • 1/4 cup Ginger bug

Instructions:

  1. Combine the water, sugar, ginger, and spices into a pot and bring to a boil. Then remove from heat and stir to dissolve the sugar. Cover and let cool completely.
  2. Add in the ginger bug and stir to combine. Then distribute evenly into 2 16 oz flip-top bottles. Fill the rest of the remaining space of the bottles with cranberry juice. Let ferment for 3-7 days, or until carbonated to preference. Then refrigerate and serve cold over ice.
