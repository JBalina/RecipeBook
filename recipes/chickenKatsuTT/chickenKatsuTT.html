Chicken Katsu

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Chicken cut into slices
  • Milk
  • 50 grams Cornstarch
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Pound chicken pieces until flat.
  2. Submerge the chicken in milk and let soak for 30 minutes.
  3. While the chicken is saoking, mix together the cornstarch and egg to form a batter.
  4. After soaking for 30 minutes, pat the chicken dry and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Batter the chicken pieces in the egg and cornstarch mixture.
  6. Then dip them on both sides in panko breadcrumbs.
  7. Heat up the oil and fry each piece for 3 minutes on each side over medium heat
