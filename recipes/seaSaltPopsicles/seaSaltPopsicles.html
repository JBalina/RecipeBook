Sea Salt Popsicles

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 3-5 drops Teal food coloring

Instructions:

  1. Place 1/2 cup each of the milk and heavy cream into a bowl and whisk together with the cornstarch.
  2. Pour the rest of the milk and heavy cream into a pot and bring to a simmer over medium-low heat. As it comes to a simmer, whisk in the sugar. Then, pour the cornstarch milk into the heating pot while whisking constantly until it thickens, about 1 minute.
  3. Whisk in the salt and then remove from heat. Strain the mixture into a heat proof bowl and then whisk in the food coloring. Cover and transfer to the fridge to chill completely.
  4. Once chilled, pour into an already churning ice cream maker. Let churn for 25 minutes. Once a soft serve consistency, transfer into popsicle molds using a piping bag. Transfer to a freezer to firm up overnight. Serve frozen.
