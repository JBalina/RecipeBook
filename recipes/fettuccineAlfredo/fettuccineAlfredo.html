Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 12 tbsp Butter
  • 1 lb Dried Fettuccine
  • 1 1/2 cup Parmesan
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp Salt, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add in the pasta and cook until al dente, about 11-12 minutes.
  2. While the pasta heats, gently melt the butter over medium heat in a large pan.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium low. Strain the pasta from the pot and place into the pan with the butter. Season with 1/2 teaspoon salt and toss well.
  4. Add parmesan and a ladles of hot pasta water incrementally, tossing the pasta continuously or mixing aggressively with tongs to incorporate the cheese.
  5. Taste the pasta and adjust the salt as necessary. Serve immediately.
