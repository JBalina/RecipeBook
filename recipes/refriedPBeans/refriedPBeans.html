Refried Pinto Beans

Ingredients:

  • 2 15oz cans Pinto beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tbsp Lard or Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1/4 tsp Salt + more to taste
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/2 Lime, juiced
  • 2 tbsp Cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat the fat in a saucepan over medium heat. Add in the onions and salt and sauté until translucent, 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add in the garlic, cumin, and chili powder and sauté for 30 more seconds. Stir in the beans and water. Let cook, covered, for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the cover and reduce to low heat. Mash some of the beans to preference until it reaches a desired consistency. Then stir and let simmer for 3 minutes or until thickened to preference.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the lime juice and cilantro. Taste and season with salt and pepper as desired. Serve with tortillas, toast or any bread.
