Wojapi

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Maple syrup OR Honey, or to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add the berries and water to a saucepan. Bring to a simmer and then reduce the heat to low.
  2. Let cook until the berries completely break down and the sauce has thickened to preference, about 30-60 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in the sweetener. Serve over corn cakes, ice creams, or meats.
