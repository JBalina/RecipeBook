Café De Olla (Mexican Coffee)

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Water
  • 3 oz Piloncillo
  • 1 stick Cinnamon
  • Optional: 3 inches Orange peel
  • Optional: 1 Star anise or 1 tsp Anise seeds
  • 4 tbsp Dark roast ground coffee

Instructions:

  1. Place the water, piloncillo, cinnamon, and any other flavorings into a large saucepan. Bring to a simmer and stir until the piloncillo is completely dissolved.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and then remove from heat. Stir in the coffee to combine.
  3. Cover pot and let steep for 5 minutes. Pour through a strainer to serve.
