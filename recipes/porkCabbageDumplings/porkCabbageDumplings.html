Tuscan Gnocchi

Ingredients:

  • Steamed dumpling wrappers
  • 1 lb Ground pork
  • 2 tbsp Dried shrimp, about 16 shrimp, soaked in 4 tbsp water until soft
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp White pepper
  • 1 Napa cabbage, diced
  • 4 Scallions, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp Sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Mince the dried shrimp when soft. Combine the pork, shrimp, shrimp water, ginger, light soy sauce, wine, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly and let marinate overnight.
  2. Reserve 6 to 8 cabbage leaves for steaming before dicing. Dice the cabbage and place into a colander mixed with 1/2 tsp salt. Let rest for 15 minutes. Then try to squeeze out as much water out of the cabbage as possible.
  3. Just before making the dumplings, mix in the cabbage, scallions, and sesame oil into the filling mixture.
  4. Line the steamer with the cabbage leaves. Wrap the filling into dumpling wrapper with any preferred wrapping method and place into the steamer. Let steam for 10 - 15 minutes.
