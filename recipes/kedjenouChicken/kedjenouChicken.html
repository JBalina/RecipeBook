Kedjenou Chicken

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • 4-5 lb Chicken thighs, skin-on, bone-in
  • 2 Red onions, diced
  • 2 Scallions, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp Garlic, grated
  • 2 sprigs Thyme
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 2-3 Scotch bonnet or habanero chilies, whole, stabbed all around with a fork
  • 1 tsp Smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp Chicken bouillon
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • 1 Red bell pepper or Green bell pepper, or half red half green, diced
  • 4 medium Tomatoes, diced

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients into a container and let marinate overnight.
  2. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Add the chicken along with the marinade and the remaining ingredients to a dutch oven. Mix to combine and place cover. Bake in the oven for about 1 hour, shaking the pot once or twice throughout cooking without removing the lid.
  4. Remove from the oven and serve immediately.
