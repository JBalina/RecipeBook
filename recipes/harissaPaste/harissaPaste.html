Harissa Paste

Ingredients:

  • 7 Dried new mexico chilies, seeded and stemmed
  • 1 6 oz jar Roasted red peppers, drained and patted dry
  • 4 cloves Garlic, peeled
  • 2 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Caraway seeds, toasted and then ground
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Ground coriander
  • 1 tsp Smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne
  • 1/8 tsp Salt, or to taste
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil + more as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak the dried chilies in hot water for at least 30 minutes, or until rehydrated.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients except the olive oil into a food processor. Then start the processor. Slowly drizzle in the 2 tbsp olive oil as it blends. Let it blend for a few minutes, or until it becomes a smooth paste.
  3. Transfer to a jar and cover with a thin layer of olive oil. Transfer to fridge to store.
